============================================================
THE REFLECTION
============================================================

You see yourself.

Not in a mirror. Not on a screen.
Outside your ship. In the Fold.

Through the viewport of another vessel.
Sitting in a cockpit identical to yours.
Looking back at you.

[Press Enter...]

Your hand moves to adjust heading.

They move at the same time.
Same gesture. Same precision.

You freeze.

They freeze.

You're staring at yourself across impossible space.

[Press Enter...]

The other you raises a hand.

Slowly. Deliberately.

A wave.

Or a warning.

Your instruments still insist: CONTACTS: 0

But you can see them. Clear as day.

Another you. Another ship. Another crossing.

All happening right now.

[Press Enter...]

They're waiting.

Watching.

What do you do?
