You back away slowly from the channel.

Cut communications. Increase distance. Retreat.

"I understand," your voice says from the channel before you close it.

"You're not ready yet."

"You will be. Eventually."

"We'll be here."

"We're always here."

The channel closes.

But you can still see them through the viewport.

All those ships. All those versions of you.

Waiting.

Patient.

They have all the time in the world.

Because time doesn't work here.
