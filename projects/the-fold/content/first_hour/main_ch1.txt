============================================================
FOLD TRANSIT - HOUR 01:00
============================================================

You've been in the Fold for one hour.

The viewport shows... something. Not space. Not darkness.
Geometric patterns that twist your eyes. Colors that don't
have names. A nauseating sense of depth where depth shouldn't exist.

Your stomach churns, but you keep it down.
'Normal psychological responses,' the handbook said.

Navigation check:

INSTRUMENTS: Course nominal. ETA 5 hours.
VIEWPORT: The destination star is... behind you?
YOUR GUT: Something is wrong.

Calibration drift. Has to be. Right?

You need to make a navigation decision.
